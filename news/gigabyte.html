Gigabyte Launches New X870E and X870 Motherboards

Gigabyte has recently unveiled the next generation of its X870E and X870 series motherboards. These AM5 platform motherboards are specifically designed to be compatible with AMD Ryzen 9000 processors, utilizing the Zen 5 microarchitecture to deliver outstanding AI processing power. Below, we explore the specifications and features of these new Gigabyte motherboards.

VRM Modules and Power Delivery

The Gigabyte X870E and X870 motherboards are equipped with powerful VRM modules that include high-quality MOSFETs, chokes, and capacitors. These modules manage CPU voltage in 20 phases, providing the required power for Ryzen 9000 processors.

AI Overclocking and DDR5 Optimization

An exciting feature highlighted by techpowerup is the support for Gigabyte's proprietary AORUS AI SNATCH technology. This AI-powered system allows users to overclock their systems with just one click, optimizing the performance of DDR5 memory at speeds of up to 8600 MT/s. The AI engine enhances system performance by improving multitasking capabilities.

Temperature Control and System Stability

Gigabyte's new technologies can reduce motherboard temperatures by up to 10 degrees Celsius, ensuring system stability during gaming sessions. Additionally, these motherboards support EZ-Latch technology, making PC case assembly easier than ever.

EZ-Latch and Connectivity Features

EZ-Latch technology includes M.2 EZ-Latch Click, M.2 EZ-Latch Plus, and PCIe EZ-Latch Plus, simplifying the process of assembling the motherboard. Furthermore, the boards feature USB4 Type-C ports with up to 40 Gbps bandwidth and support for Wi-Fi 7 for ultra-fast connectivity.

Advanced Cooling and Debugging

Gigabyte has introduced several cooling innovations to keep these motherboards running efficiently. The boards are built with an eight-layer PCB, similar to server-grade motherboards. In addition, the EZ-Debug Zone includes various LEDs that display system statuses, making the troubleshooting process easier.

PCIe EZ-Latch and Tool-Free Installation

The PCIe EZ-Latch Plus allows users to install a graphics card without needing any screws, while M.2 EZ-Latch Click and M.2 EZ-Latch Plus make it possible to install SSDs and M.2 heatsinks without any tools.

BIOS Interface and Fan Control

The BIOS interface has been redesigned, allowing users to easily select different themes with a single click. Additionally, the AIO Fan Control feature enables non-Windows users to adjust the speed of their AIO fans directly in the BIOS before booting into the operating system.
