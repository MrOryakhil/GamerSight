The Legend of Zelda: Echoes of Wisdom Starts Strong in Japan

In the latest gaming news, *The Legend of Zelda: Echoes of Wisdom* has had a powerful start in Japan. According to *Famitsu* statistics reported by *GamingBolt*, the latest *Zelda* title topped Japan’s weekly physical software sales charts with over 200,000 copies sold. For comparison, the *The Legend of Zelda: Link’s Awakening* remake sold over 140,000 copies in its first week in Japan.

However, *The Legend of Zelda: Echoes of Wisdom* isn’t the only game making waves in Japan. *The Legend of Heroes: Kai no Kiseki – Farewell, O Zemuria* sold a total of 47,000 units, placing it among the top sellers for the week. The PlayStation 5 version of the game sold over 29,000 units, ranking second, while the PlayStation 4 version sold 17,000 copies, placing third.

Additionally, *EA Sports FC 25* secured multiple spots on the sales charts. The Nintendo Switch version, PlayStation 5 version, and PlayStation 4 version ranked fourth, fifth, and seventh respectively, with total sales exceeding 32,000 copies. Meanwhile, *Astro Bot* claimed the sixth spot.
