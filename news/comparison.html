This year has been an exceptional one for fans of multiplayer third-person shooters. Two major titles, Space Marine 2 and Helldivers 2, launched for consoles and PC, each attracting hundreds of thousands of active players. Both games share many similarities in terms of gameplay, story themes, world-building, and multiplayer components. In this article, we compare Helldivers 2 with Space Marine 2 to determine which game stands out.

Comparison of Helldivers 2 vs. Space Marine 2

Earlier this year, Sony released one of the best multiplayer games in recent years, Helldivers 2, for PC and PlayStation 5. In this game, players team up with friends to complete various missions aimed at spreading freedom and democracy across the galaxy. Despite receiving some harsh criticism from fans due to Sony’s recent policies, the game’s quality remains intact.

Games Similar to Helldivers 2

On the other hand, Saber Interactive released a new entry in the Space Marine series, which, like Helldivers 2, was met with widespread fan approval. This third-person shooter features both offline and online modes that can keep players engaged for hours. Its story is also notable, with the game set in the rich world of Warhammer 40,000. Next, we’ll compare various aspects of Helldivers 2 and Space Marine 2 to see which game fares better.

Graphics Comparison: Helldivers 2 vs. Space Marine 2

The first area of comparison is the graphics and visual effects of these two games. This is one of the few areas where both games excel and can be considered equal. Both Helldivers 2 and Space Marine 2 emphasize realistic graphics, striving for excellence in detail.

Whether in environmental visuals or character and enemy design, both games perform admirably. One key factor in their visual appeal is the impressive lighting, especially when transitioning between night and day stages. Environmentally, both games offer relatively similar settings, mostly featuring large, open areas where lighting plays a crucial role.

Regarding additional visual effects like enemy deaths or the muzzle flashes from player weapons, both developers have done an excellent job. Since both games focus heavily on fast-paced multiplayer gameplay, there are limits to the level of detail that can be expected. Nonetheless, both Helldivers 2 and Space Marine 2 are visually top-tier, making it difficult to declare a definitive winner in this category.

World-Building and Atmosphere Comparison

The first area where Space Marine 2 pulls ahead of Helldivers 2 is in world-building and atmosphere. The Warhammer 40,000 universe is so rich that one could spend weeks or months researching it and still have more to uncover. This deep world-building gives Space Marine 2 an edge over Helldivers 2 in terms of character development and story richness.

However, Helldivers 2, as an original and non-adapted title, also offers solid world-building. The players’ fight for freedom and the game’s evolution through updates is engaging. However, over time, the game’s world starts to feel repetitive, and it becomes apparent that there is little new to offer in terms of narrative or world-building. The world and atmosphere of Helldivers 2 are comparatively shallow.

One area where Space Marine 2 excels is in character development, especially for secondary characters. In Helldivers 2, the soldiers you choose have no distinct identities and are merely tools for fighting in a war for ideology. In contrast, the key characters in Space Marine 2 have backstories that make players feel more connected to them. The dialogue between secondary characters also fits perfectly within the game's world, enhancing its immersive experience.

Customization Comparison: Helldivers 2 vs. Space Marine 2

Space Marine 2 significantly outshines Helldivers 2 in the area of character customization, particularly in multiplayer mode. Players have more freedom to design and customize every aspect of their character’s armor, including diverse color schemes, unique designs, and various models.

In contrast, the customization options in Helldivers 2 are limited to a pre-set selection of armor, helmets, and capes. Players can’t even change the color of their armor or mix different armor sets. In Space Marine 2, players can easily use different colors for each part of their armor and even mix components from different sets, giving them more freedom to create a unique look. Thus, in the customization department, Space Marine 2 is deeper and offers more freedom than Helldivers 2.

Weapon Variety Comparison

When it comes to weapon variety, Helldivers 2 has a clear edge over Space Marine 2. In Space Marine 2, the player is mostly limited to different versions of a single weapon, the Boltgun, with a few other weapons available. Once you’ve gotten used to one weapon, there’s little incentive to change.

In contrast, Helldivers 2 offers a large variety of weapons, such as pistols, shotguns, and machine guns, each catering to different playstyles. Players will spend time experimenting with different weapons to find the ones that suit them best. Additionally, Helldivers 2 features special abilities called "Strategems," which allow players to select different power-ups, like airstrikes, during the game. While Space Marine 2 has a similar feature, it lacks the variety offered by Helldivers 2. Overall, in terms of weapon variety, Helldivers 2 performs better and offers a deeper experience.

Previously, we noted that both games excel in visual appeal. However, in terms of environment and map design, Helldivers 2 has more variety, featuring battles on different planets. That said, many of the environments in the long missions (which can last 40 minutes each) feel repetitive.

In contrast, Space Marine 2 is filled with environmental details such as different types of buildings and expansive combat zones. These environmental details often contribute to the game’s storytelling. For example, during a mission, players might encounter the corpses of their teammates, indicating that enemies had previously passed through the area. These subtle details give Space Marine 2 the edge in environment and map design.

Music Comparison: Helldivers 2 vs. Space Marine 2

One area that often receives less attention is the music. While this is a crucial element for setting the tone in different moments of the game, it’s frequently overlooked. The Warhammer 40,000 universe is known for its epic and engaging music, but Space Marine 2 lacks memorable tracks. Even after hours of gameplay, recalling a particular theme or motif is difficult.

Helldivers 2, though featuring fewer tracks, makes better use of them. The main menu music, for instance, becomes iconic over time, and hearing even a snippet of it immediately brings the game to mind. This makes Helldivers 2 more memorable in terms of music, giving it an edge over Space Marine 2.

Gameplay Comparison: Helldivers 2 vs. Space Marine 2

Finally, the most crucial aspect of any game—gameplay. Both games feature distinct gameplay structures, yet they are similar in their overall approach. In both, players, either solo or with friends, enter maps, fight waves of enemies, and complete missions.

Both developers have recognized that gameplay is the key element of these titles and have focused heavily on making it enjoyable. As a result, neither game has a clear advantage in terms of gameplay quality and mechanics. Though there are some differences, these are primarily designed to make each game unique, not to suggest that one is better than the other.

Even when comparing the multiplayer modes of Space Marine 2 and Helldivers 2, both games excel. The balance of weapons, enemy numbers, and the strategic thinking required to defeat foes are highlights of both. The fact that both games successfully maintain player engagement and prevent boredom through repetitive gameplay loops is a testament to their success.

Final Verdict: Space Marine 2

The latest entry from Saber Interactive and the Warhammer 40,000 franchise stands out due to its attention to details like environment design, customization, and its rich world. Though it falls short in areas like weapon variety compared to Helldivers 2, overall Space Marine 2 delivers a better experience in the categories mentioned.

That said, Helldivers 2 remains a respectable title and one of the best games of 2024 alongside Space Marine 2. Ultimately, personal taste plays a key role in determining which game a player enjoys more. In this article, we aimed to provide an unbiased comparison of both titles to see which one, at least in a direct comparison, emerges as the superior game
