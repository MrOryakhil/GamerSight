Face the North Yankton Nightmare in GTA Online's Ludendorff Cemetery Survival Mode!

Survive the Undead and Earn Big Rewards in This Halloween Event

Get ready to battle the undead in the chilling hills of North Yankton! For the first time in GTA Online, players can return to Ludendorff Cemetery to fight terrifying zombies, juggernauts, and possessed animals in the new Ludendorff Cemetery Survival Mode. This limited-time mode is packed with thrills, offering 2X GTA$ and RP through October 23, along with exclusive rewards for those who survive the horror.

Survive the Nightmare and Earn Exclusive Rewards

Head to the Ludendorff Cemetery and endure three waves of relentless enemies to win the Gray Creepy Cat Mask and GTA$100,000 as part of this week’s challenge. GTA+ members get even more — take on the zombies to unlock the Mummy Outfit, delivered within 72 hours!

Join the North Yankton Nightmare Community Challenge

GTA Online is also hosting a Community Challenge, tasking players with collectively eliminating 80 million zombies in Ludendorff Cemetery Survival. If the goal is reached, all players will unlock the Orange Glow Skeleton Onesie and the Red Hooded Skull Mask. Track your progress and find out more on the GTA Online website.

More Halloween Events and Bonuses

Halloween brings more spooky rewards with 2X GTA$ and RP in Ghosts Exposed, where you can hunt down ghosts haunting the streets of Los Santos. Capture all 10 apparitions to earn the Ghosts Exposed Outfit and Ghosts Exposed Livery.

Be on the lookout for UFO sightings starting October 15 and snap pictures of them for Omega to earn extra cash and RP!

Triple Rewards on Halloween Community Series
