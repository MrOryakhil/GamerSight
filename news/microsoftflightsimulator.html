Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 System Requirements Revealed: Get Ready for a Smoother Experience

Microsoft has officially unveiled the system requirements for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 on PC, and there's great news for fans of the series. The game will require significantly less storage space than its predecessor—just 50 GB. This is a big improvement considering the size of previous versions.

However, the recommended setup suggests 32 GB of RAM, while an ideal experience will require a whopping 64 GB, which is more than what most gamers currently have installed on their PCs.

According to Sebastian Wloch, CEO and co-founder of Asobo Studio, another major improvement in Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 is the reduced bandwidth usage, making the game more accessible to a wider range of users. Below, you'll find the full system requirements:

System Requirements for Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024:

Component Minimum Specs Recommended Specs Ideal Specs
OS Windows 10/11/(64-bit) Windows 10/11/(64-bit) Windows 10/11/(64-bit)
DirectX Version 12 Version 12 Version 12
Processor Ryzen 5 2600X or Core i7-6800K Ryzen 7 2700X or Core i7-10700K Ryzen 7 7900X or Core i7-14700K
Graphics RX 5700 or GTX 970 RX 5700 XT or RTX 2080 RX 7900 XT or RTX 4080
RAM 16GB 32GB 64GB
Storage 50GB 50GB 50GB
Bandwidth 10 Mbps 50 Mbps 100 Mbps

Mark your calendars—Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024 will launch on November 19, 2024, for Xbox Series X|S and PC. As always with Microsoft titles, it will be available on Xbox Game Pass from day one, offering even more value to subscribers.

Whether you're a seasoned pilot or new to the skies, the new improvements in performance and bandwidth usage make this the perfect time to take flight in Microsoft Flight Simulator 2024.
