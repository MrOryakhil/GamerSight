NVIDIA RTX 5090 & 5080: Specifications, Features, and CES 2025 Launch

Yesterday, the specifications for the high-end RTX 50 series graphics cards based on Blackwell architecture, including the GeForce RTX 5090 and GeForce RTX 5080, were leaked. New details regarding their design and release timeframe have now been revealed, which we will examine below.

According to Wccftech, the RTX 5090 and RTX 5080 graphics cards are set to debut at CES 2025. This aligns with earlier reports from renowned analyst Kopite7kimi. Additionally, a special China-market edition of the RTX 5090, named RTX 5090D, is expected to launch within the same timeframe.

Alongside the desktop RTX 50 series cards, it is also anticipated that laptop versions of this series will be announced, with CES 2025 showcasing laptops powered by these cards and the latest AMD and Intel processors.

Key Specifications of RTX 5090

Specification Details
Architecture Blackwell
PCB Layers 14
Memory 16 GDDR7 Modules, 3GB per module
Memory Speed 28-32 Gbps
Total Memory Bandwidth Over 2TB/s
Cooling Design Compact dual-slot cooler (Founders Edition)
PCIe Support PCIe 5.0
DisplayPort DisplayPort 2.1a

Cooling Design in Founders Edition

NVIDIA appears to have learned from past experience and plans to reduce the physical size of its flagship graphics cards. The RTX 5090 will feature a new dual-slot cooling design that is more compact than previous generations.

It is still unclear if the RTX 5080 will utilize the same cooling system, but it is expected to have a different appearance compared to earlier models.

PCIe 5.0 and DisplayPort 2.1a Support

One of the shortcomings of the RTX 4090 was its lack of support for the latest DisplayPort standards. However, the RTX 50 series will support both PCIe 5.0 and DisplayPort 2.1a, making these the first graphics cards to adopt these standards.

Support for DisplayPort 2.1a will enable the RTX 50 series to connect to 16K HDR displays with a 60Hz refresh rate, opening up new possibilities for ultra-high-definition gaming and professional uses.
