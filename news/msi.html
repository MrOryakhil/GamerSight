MSI Introduces Force GC300 Wireless Gaming Controller

MSI has recently unveiled the Force GC300 wireless gaming controller, focusing on customization capabilities, diverse connectivity options, and compatibility with a wide range of gaming devices. Read on for more details.

According to Guru3d, the Force GC300 controller boasts broad compatibility with devices such as PCs, Xbox, and PlayStation. It supports not only the Xbox Series X and PlayStation 5 but also older versions of these consoles.

The Force GC300 gaming controller features a 600mAh battery, providing up to 20 hours of gameplay on a single charge. The design includes a standard layout with two analog sticks, four standard buttons on the right, two trigger button sets, and a 4-pole audio jack for headphone connection. The controller measures 148x111x68 mm and weighs 230 grams.

One of the standout features of this controller is its programmable rear buttons, which can be customized for different games using the MSI Center software. This allows users to tailor the controller to their preferences and gaming style. Additionally, a key on the back of the controller enables users to adjust the trigger button travel distance in two stages, enhancing precision and adaptability in games.

The MSI Force GC300 comes with three connectivity options: wireless 2.4GHz, Bluetooth, and wired USB. This versatility ensures compatibility with PCs, consoles, and even mobile devices. The 2.4GHz wireless connection is ideal for fast-paced games requiring quick responses, while Bluetooth offers convenient everyday use. The USB option provides a reliable connection when wireless conditions are poor and allows for simultaneous charging.

The product is expected to launch on October 10th at an approximate price of $55.
