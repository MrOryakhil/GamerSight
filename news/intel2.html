Intel Reveals Prices for Xeon 6900P Granite Rapids Processors

A while ago, Intel unveiled its Granite Rapids processors under the Xeon 6900P brand. Now, the prices for these chips have been announced, and they are astonishing. Intel has stated that the flagship 128-core Xeon 6980P from the Xeon 6900P Granite Rapids series will be priced at $17,800!

The new generation of Intel processors comes with innovative features and a core count similar to AMD's recent chips. The Xeon 6900P, codenamed Granite Rapids, has been launched to compete directly with AMD EPYC.

The Granite Rapids Xeon 6900P data center processors from Intel were launched a week ago, promising a 90% performance improvement over the previous 5th generation Xeon processors.

Intel has now revealed the official prices for each chip in this family. The Granite Rapids series adds four processors to the Xeon 6900P family, ranging from 72 cores to 128 cores.

The Intel Xeon 6900P Granite Rapids processors are built using Intel's Intel 3 technology and feature Redwood Cove P-Cores and IMC, while the I/O section is built with Intel 7 technology, incorporating I/O controllers and accelerator engines.

The Xeon 6900P Granite Rapids-P processors will be the first to support Multiplexed Rank DIMMs (MRDIMMs), offering memory speeds of up to 8800 MT/s, a significant improvement over DDR5-6400.

Finally, the Xeon 6980P processor doubles the core and thread count and offers 504MB of cache with a maximum power consumption of 500 watts. It is claimed that the Xeon 6980P brings major improvements in AI performance.
