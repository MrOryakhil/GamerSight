KLEVV Introduces New GENUINE G560 and CRAS C715 SSDs

KLEVV, a company that has recently gained significant popularity, even in our country, has introduced two new SSDs aimed at both high-end and budget PCs. Stay with us as we introduce these two storage devices.

The first product, named GENUINE G560 Gen 5.0 SSD, is designed for high-end systems, offering impressive speed.

This SSD uses the Gen5x4 NVMe on M.2 slot, with sequential read and write speeds reaching 14,000 MB/s and 12,000 MB/s, respectively, making it one of the fastest SSDs on the market.

It is equipped with 23x-Layer 3D NAND Flash technology, providing optimal efficiency alongside 4K random speed of 1,400K IOPS. The SSD will be available in 1TB, 2TB, and 4TB capacities, with a TBW of 3000, ensuring years of reliable use.

Visually, this SSD features a white and black aluminum heatsink for cooling and aesthetic appeal. According to the manufacturer, this SSD also supports Microsoft DirectStorage, reducing game load times by up to 70% and lowering CPU usage by up to 99%.

The second product, introduced for budget-friendly systems, is the CRAS C715 Gen 3 SSD, using PCI-E Gen 3x4 interface. Its sequential read and write speeds reach 3200 MB/s and 2000 MB/s, respectively. It will be available in 256GB, 512GB, and 1TB capacities.

KLEVV has incorporated SLC technology with 3D NAND Flash in this product for optimal performance on the older Gen 3 interface, alongside LPDC ECC for enhanced durability and reliability.

Unfortunately, the company has not yet announced the prices for these SSDs, but both will be available for purchase in reputable online stores starting from October.
