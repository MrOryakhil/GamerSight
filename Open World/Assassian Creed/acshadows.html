Assassian Creed: Shadows

Introduction

We all know assassin’s creed games for the Amazing storyline, immersive gameplay and of course unforgettable places it took us like from the Holy land (Israel, Palestine and Syria) in the first installment to the rooftops of Baghdad in the latest one Ac Mirage.

Today we are going to discuss everything about the upcoming title Assassins creed shadows in this beloved installment from release date to gameplay available platforms and setting and more.

So be with us until the end of this Journey

Watch the Official Assassian Creed: Shadows Trailer

Release Date

AC shadows have been in development for some time until after Assassin’s creed mirage was released, a data leak showed that the next installment is in the works with the code name red, then Ubisoft has confirmed that the Ac Shadows is going to release in November 15.

And as always you can play it 3 days early if you buy one of gold or ultimate editions.

PlatForms

Assassin’s creed shadows is coming for Xbox series X|S, PlayStation 5 and Pc

AC Shadows Setting

This time Ubisoft is going to take us to the Feudal japan We will be welcomed with glorious Castle towns beautiful shrines and amazing landscapes.

And not to forget unpredictable weather and seasons change and reactive environment.

Game is set in central japan so we can visit known cities like Osaka, Kyoto... also we can visit the temples and shrines that are present in real world.

AC Shadows characters

Assassin’s creed shadows will feature two main characters Yasuke and Naoe.From the start they are presented full opposite giving us a two different experience.

Yasuke is a powerful African Samurai which is enslaved by Portuguese and transferred to japan.He is a powerful man he quickly gets involved by noticeable figures of the time.

On the other hand, we have Naoe a Shinobi Assassin (Write more ) who have skills of parkour stealth and assassination.

AC Shadows Gameplay

Assassin’s creed shadows is going to be an expansive open world action adventure RPG set in feudal japan.

While the last entry in the series (Assassin’s creed mirage) was a “back to the roots of Ac franchise” with more intention to stealth and assassination, Assassin’s creed shadows is going to go to the way of AC origins, odyssey and Valhalla with the expanded RPG elements.

Conclusion:

With the release of "Assassin's Creed Shadows" set for November 15, 2023, anticipation gets higher and higher. This RPG, set in feudal Japan, promises to deliver innovative gameplay, two memorable characters, breathtaking landscapes, and a world shaped by dynamic climate changes and destructible environments.

It’s shaping up to be a standout entry in the "Assassin's Creed" series, offering new experiences that could redefine the franchise
